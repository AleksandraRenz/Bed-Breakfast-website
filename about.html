Blue Jay Bed & Breakfast



Set in the beautiful Scottish Highland Town of Oban,
The Blue Jay Guest House has welcomed visitors to Oban
for the past 10 years.

About Us

The Bed and Breakfast is a small cosy guest house 2 minutes walk from the harbour. Guests can enjoy our on site bar stocked with a wide range of local drinks. Free private parking is available on site. All rooms are equipped with a 'micro kitchen' that comprises of fridge, microwave, toaster, kettle, crockery and cutlery (tea, coffee, sugar and milk provided). Flatscreen Colour T.V. with Freeview, central heating, hairdryer and alarm clock.

Local Area

There is a local swimming pool a 10 minute walk from the guest house. There is a wide range of bars and restaurants in the town centre including the famous multi-award winning seafood restaurant Kelvins. There are also many boutique and craft shops around the town.
