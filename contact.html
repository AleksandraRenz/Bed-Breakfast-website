Blue Jay Bed & Breakfast



Set in the beautiful Scottish Highland Town of Oban,
The Blue Jay Guest House has welcomed visitors to Oban
for the past 10 years.

The guest house is located close to the town centre of Craic.
The address is:

123 Flemington Street
Craic
Q12 3YZ


  • From Glasgow, follow the M100 to the Kelvinshire Roundabout then follow the A123 for 19 miles to Craic.
  • From Edinburgh, follow the M200 to the Kelvinshire Roundabout then follow the A123 for 19 miles to Craic.
